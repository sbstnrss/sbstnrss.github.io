Info

I'm a Designer and Product Manager living and working in Hamburg. I grew up in Stuttgart and studied Industrial Design (Bachelor of Arts) at Pforzheim University School of Design. My passion for design, technology and illustration is reflected in my work. The main aspect of my work is technical and aesthetic problem solving. I'm driven by the idea of staying open minded and mixing up genres. If you are interested in collaboration or you have any questions or comments feel free to contact me! I'm available for Freelance.
